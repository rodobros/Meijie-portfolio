Kiehl's

Kiehl’s KCR is an iPad app that helps store associates to become product experts and build deeper connections with customers.

Roles: Information Architecture, UX/UI Design, Prototype.

Tools: Sketch, InVision, Zeplin, Keynote, Google Sheet, Jira.

Duration: Dec 2019

The Challenge

Currently, Kiehl’s associates ask a series of skincare related questions and use spot tests to find a customer’s skin type. To make finding a customer’s skin type faster, Kiehl’s has come up with a new digital tool, I helped them to integrate the new skin assessment tool into their tablet app. Other challenges includes: designing an event and a contact list features for sales associates to effectively schedule and follow up with customers and events.

HSA Feature

Due to NDA, I will only include some screens of Healthy skin assessment (HSA) feature. This feature allows associates to help customers analyze their skin health and provide personalized product recommendations.
